about us

Our story began more than a decade ago. Relying on proven domestic and Viennese experiences in making cakes such as Esterhazy and Zacher cakes, we offered our fellow citizens a variety of cakes, pastries, ice cream and cups. Italian recipes based on cream and chocolate have supplemented the existing assortment with new aromas, flavors and shapes. This is how the unique offer of the Sweet Heart confectionery was created, modified according to local tastes.
